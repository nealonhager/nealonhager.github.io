THINK GLOBALLY. ACT LOCALLY.

The community supports us and we show our support in return.

People ask why we wanted to become a Patagonia store. How could we not? In our ten years of experience, we found that Patagonia is the last outdoor sports company that is true to them themselves and to the athletes they support. They are still independently owned. They still put the athlete first. They support women's sports as thoroughly as they support men's sports. They don't leave anyone out in the cold. They stand behind their products, their dealers and their customers—one hundred percent. . . . Becoming a Patagonia store was a very easy decision for us.

Why buy locally? We take great pride in being a locally owned and operated business. The community supports us and we show our support in return. We are proud to donate to over 50 local charities per year. Spending money locally supports local employees and the community of Bend as a whole.

So what’s our philosophy?

That's easy. Work hard. Provide an outstanding service and product to our customers. Treat our employees well. Play hard.
